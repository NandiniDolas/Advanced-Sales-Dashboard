# Advanced-Sales-Dashboard

Advanced Sales Dashboard – Power BI Project
📌 Project Overview
The Advanced Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze sales performance across multiple business dimensions such as revenue, profit, product categories, and regional performance.
The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales trends, profitability, and product performance, enabling better business decision-making.

🎯 Project Objectives
The main goals of this project were:
•	Analyze overall sales and profit performance
•	Track monthly revenue trends
•	Evaluate profit margin and sales growth
•	Identify top-performing products
•	Compare regional sales performance
•	Analyze category-level contribution to revenue

📂 Dataset
The dataset used is the Superstore Sales Dataset, which contains transactional sales data including:
•	Order ID
•	Order Date
•	Region
•	Category
•	Sub-Category
•	Product Name
•	Sales
•	Profit
•	Quantity
•	Discount
•	Ship Mode
This dataset allows detailed analysis at both transactional and aggregated levels.

🛠 Tools & Technologies
•	Power BI
•	DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
•	Data Modeling
•	Interactive Data Visualization

📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks the following business metrics:
•	Total Orders
•	Total Revenue
•	Total Profit
•	Profit Margin (%)
•	Sales Growth (%)
These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

📈 Dashboard Features
1️⃣ Revenue & Profit Trend
A combined chart showing monthly revenue and profit trends, helping identify seasonality and performance fluctuations.
2️⃣ Regional Performance Analysis
Bar charts comparing sales and profit across regions to highlight geographic performance differences.
3️⃣ Category Contribution
A donut chart visualizing the revenue share of each product category, helping identify key business drivers.
4️⃣ Top 10 Products
A ranking chart identifying the top-performing products based on revenue.
5️⃣ Interactive Filtering
Users can dynamically filter the dashboard using slicers such as:
•	Year
•	Region
•	Category

🔍 Key Business Insights
Some insights derived from the dashboard include:
•	Sales show strong year-over-year growth, indicating business expansion.
•	The West region generates the highest revenue, contributing significantly to overall sales.
•	Technology category leads revenue share, making it the primary business driver.
•	A small group of products contributes a large portion of total revenue, indicating product concentration.
•	Profit margins suggest potential opportunities for cost optimization or pricing improvements.
